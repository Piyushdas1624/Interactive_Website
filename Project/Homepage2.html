About Suprapa High School

Suprapa High School was founded on April 15, 1995, in Suprapa Nagar by a group of visionary educators. "Suprapa" is a Sanskrit word meaning "Good Beginning" and "Ad Astra per Suprapa" means "To the stars through a good beginning". Starting from humble origins, our school has blossomed into a premier educational institution.
After years of dedicated effort, we proudly operate from our spacious and modern campus spanning approximately 2 acres in Suprapa Nagar. Our school has evolved from its foundational years to become a beacon of academic excellence, holistic development, and cultural values. We pride ourselves on combining traditional educational values with modern teaching methodologies to prepare students for the challenges of the future. Read more about our rich history and vision

From the Principal's Desk


Dear Parents and Students,
It is my profound honor to serve as the Principal of Suprapa High School.

My educational philosophy centers on the belief that every child possesses unlimited potential for learning and growth. At SHS, our focus is on ensuring that each student receives personalized guidance that nurtures their unique abilities. We understand that true education isn't merely about teaching; it's about guiding, mentoring, and developing well-rounded individuals. As educators, our primary responsibility is to be dedicated developers of people who inspire the next generation.

The foundation of our students' success rests on the strong partnership between students, staff, and parents. Active parental involvement is crucial to this educational journey. Our dedicated staff creates a nurturing environment where young minds can learn, explore, and flourish. Beyond academics, we offer a comprehensive extra-curricular program every Friday, featuring diverse disciplines such as music, dance, cricket, football, and more, all under the expert guidance of accomplished teachers.

At Suprapa High, we are committed to developing confident, knowledgeable, and empathetic individuals prepared to face future challenges with resilience and integrity. We strive to create an environment where each student discovers their potential, cultivates their unique talents, and develops a lifelong passion for learning. Our goal is to equip them not just with academic knowledge but also with values that will guide them throughout life.

Mr. Subrata Chakraborty
Principal, Suprapa High School

Our Distinguished Faculty

At SHS, we take immense pride in our exceptional team of educators who form the backbone of our institution. Our faculty members are not just teachers; they are mentors, guides, and inspirations who shape the future of our students. Our teaching staff:

  • Holds advanced degrees and specialized qualifications in their respective academic disciplines.
  • Brings extensive classroom experience combined with innovative teaching methodologies.
  • Regularly participates in professional development programs, workshops, and training sessions to stay updated with the latest educational trends.
  • Demonstrates an unwavering commitment to nurturing each student's intellectual, emotional, and social development.
  • Creates an inclusive and supportive learning environment that celebrates diversity and encourages critical thinking.
  • Maintains open communication channels with parents to ensure collaborative educational journeys.

Our faculty members are selected not only for their academic credentials but also for their passion for teaching and their ability to connect with students on a personal level. They understand that education extends beyond textbooks and examinations, focusing on character development and life skills that prepare students for real-world challenges.
