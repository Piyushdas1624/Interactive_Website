Student Portal

Welcome to the Suprapa High Student Portal. Access your academic information, attendance records, grades, and other important resources here.

To access the portal, please log in using your student credentials.

Login to Student Portal

Portal Features (Upon Login):

  • View Personal Profile
  • Check Attendance Records
  • View Term-wise Grades and Report Cards
  • Access Online Study Materials provided by Teachers
  • Check Fee Payment Status
  • View School Notices and Announcements
  • Communicate with Teachers (Feature coming soon)

If you face any issues logging in or accessing the portal, please contact the school administration office or email support at support@suprapahigh.com.
